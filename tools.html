ChartRobo Educational Tools

ChartRobo provides complimentary tools to help students learn chart analysis, seasonal behavior, and reversal psychology. These tools are available for 1 month to all participants of our workshops and mentorship program for educational demonstration purposes only.

  • ChartRobo Seasonal Trend Analyzer (TradeRadar): A learning aid that studies historical data and current price movement to help participants understand recurring seasonal behaviors and trend patterns.It finds support & resistance based on price action
  • ChartRobo Potential Reversal Finder (WaveX): An analytical study tool that highlights potential reversal zones for conceptual learning about market psychology and wave structure.

    • Both tools are for educational use only — they do not generate trading signals or financial advice.

    How Students Benefit

    • Practice identifying seasonal and cyclical market behavior using historical data.
    • Learn to interpret support, resistance, and reversal concepts.
    • Enhance understanding of wave structures through visual study.
    • Apply these insights during live demo sessions under mentorship guidance.

    Why These Tools Matter

    • Completely free for enrolled students.
    • Developed for hands-on educational use within TradingView.
    • Help bridge the gap between chart psychology and analytical reasoning.
    • Encourage emotionless and structured decision-making practice.
