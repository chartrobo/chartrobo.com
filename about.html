About ChartRobo

ChartRobo – Stock Market Analysis Academy is an educational initiative founded by Brajesh Kumar, an NISM Certified Research Analyst with professional experience across banking, finance, and technology.

The academy focuses on developing structured, disciplined, and objective market understanding rather than prediction-based or emotion-driven approaches.

About the Founder/Educator

Brajesh Kumar
NISM Certified Research Analyst | Ex-Banker (Bank of Baroda)

With experience spanning banking operations, financial markets, and analytical research, Brajesh founded ChartRobo to address a key gap in market education — the lack of structured thinking and decision discipline.

His teaching approach emphasizes understanding price behavior, market structure, and time-based context, helping learners think independently rather than rely on external opinions.

Our Vision

Modern financial markets reward discipline, patience, and structure — not emotional reactions or shortcuts.

ChartRobo’s vision is to help learners develop a calm, analytical mindset while interpreting charts and market behavior objectively.

Our Journey

ChartRobo was created to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-market observation.

Over years of market study and live observation, the academy’s learning framework evolved into a structured approach that combines market structure, price behavior, psychology, and time awareness.

Our Philosophy

Markets reflect collective human behavior and follow repeatable structural patterns.

By combining psychological awareness with logical market structure, ChartRobo helps learners reduce emotional bias and develop clarity in decision-making.

We believe successful market participants must think objectively, act patiently, and follow process over emotion.

What We Offer

  • Basic Workshop: A structured foundational program focused on market understanding, price behavior, structure, and analytical discipline.
  • Live Market Analysis (YouTube): Periodic public sessions demonstrating real-time chart behavior, analytical thinking, and market structure for educational observation.

All programs and sessions are educational in nature and are designed to improve analytical understanding — not to provide trading tips, stock recommendations, or investment advice.
