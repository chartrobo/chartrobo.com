What You Will Learn

The ChartRobo Basic Workshop focuses on developing a structured and objective approach to market analysis. The emphasis is on understanding why price moves, not on predicting or memorizing setups.

  • Price Action Analysis: Understanding how price behaves through candlestick structures, rejection, acceptance, momentum, and exhaustion.
  • Candlestick & Chart Patterns: Interpreting patterns as reflections of market psychology rather than treating them as standalone signals.
  • Demand & Supply Zones: Identifying areas of institutional activity, imbalance, and potential reaction zones on charts.
  • Elliott Wave Concepts: Learning wave structure as a framework to understand market phases, corrections, and directional context.
  • Time-Based Market Analysis: Understanding how time plays a role in validating structure, momentum shifts, and corrective behavior.
  • Combining All Concepts: Integrating price action, structure, zones, wave behavior, and time analysis into a single, logical decision-making framework.

All concepts are taught using historical and live chart examples strictly for educational observation and understanding.
