About Us :

  • ChartRobo is an educational platform by Brajesh Kumar to teach and demonstrate technical analysis using custom indicators.
  • The platform will soon transition into a SEBI-registered research and analysis service after obtaining regulatory approval.
  • Our goal is to empower traders to understand market patterns and trade setups commonly studied in technical analysis, using custom-built TradingView indicators for educational purposes.
  • Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced trader, ChartRobo simplifies complex analysis into structured, easy-to-learn concepts.

Our vision is to help traders develop self-reliance by combining analytics, discipline, and structured learning.

Our Mission :

  • We aim to eliminate noise and complexity from trading by providing simple, transparent, and effective tools.
  • We empower learners with data-driven and price-action-based insights, ensuring clarity and confidence in every analysis.

Integrity, transparency, and continuous learning guide everything we do at ChartRobo.
